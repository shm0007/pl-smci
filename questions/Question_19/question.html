Scenario: You are a developer for Pizza++, a new online-only pizza delivery company. The previous developer had created a "shopping cart" system where people could create and customize orders. You've added a "purchase" component showing the price of the items in the cart, the applicable tax, and a text entry field allowing the user to add a numeric amount to tip the driver. The tip is added directly to the other costs to produce a total amount owed. A payment processing company handles charging credits cards.

Question: Which of the following is the most likely potential flaw in the system?

 People can get free pizza. The payment processor is untrustworthy. The code from the previous developer could have a hidden backdoor. An SQL Injection attack could reveal private customer data.
