Scenario: Your thirteen-year-old nephew uses his own Gmail account only to watch YouTube videos on his mother's iPad. One day he came to you and said that he put his Gmail credentials into a website in exchange for getting some in-game currency for his favorite video game. Checking out the website, you think it looks suspicious, but nothing seems wrong with your nephew's Gmail account.

Question: What should you do first after these events??

 Change the password for the account. Look for any suspicious activity in your nephew's Gmail account. Explain to your nephew why they shouldn't enter their credentials into suspicious websites in the future. Delete the Gmail account and create a new one.
