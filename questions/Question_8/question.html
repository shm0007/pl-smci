Scenario: John is the CEO of a large corporation headquartered in a typical office building.. He controls the online bank accounts for the business, using a computer that is connected only to an internal finance network (i.e., it has no Internet access or access to other employee's computers). Instead of memorizing the passwords for the accounts (which are long random strings), John wrote all of the passwords on a sticky note and placed the note in a drawer of his desk, which is in his office. The door to John's office is always locked. There are only two keys; John carries one with him at all times, and the other is locked in the administrative assistant's safe, which is bolted to the floor.

Question: Choose the most significant problem with this scenario.

 John's office is not physically secure. The passwords are written down. The passwords could be guessed. If the sticky note is lost, John will lose access to the accounts.
